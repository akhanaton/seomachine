## 2. PMT Profile

- **Trustpilot**: 3.6/5 (17 reviews -- small sample)
- **Pricing**: Free (ad-supported, no paywall, no account required)
- **Business model**: PMT Education is a registered charity/non-profit (est. 2019), separate from the original commercial site
- **Strengths**: Massive free library, past papers by topic, cheat sheets, worksheets, video solutions, Solomon Press worksheets
- **Weaknesses**: Intrusive video ads, mark schemes only (no worked solutions with reasoning), site crashes during exam periods, typos in content, overwhelming/unstructured navigation, **zero CIE/Cambridge 9709 content** -- entirely Edexcel/AQA/OCR focused
- **Student sentiment**: "Best free resource but terrible ads" and "wish it had CIE content" (The Student Room)

### The Critical Gap
PMT is the #1 recommended free resource in student forums. But it has **zero Cambridge International content**. CIE students use it by adapting Edexcel questions -- which works for pure maths but means no 9709-specific exam tips, no CIE mark scheme analysis, and no examiner report intelligence.
