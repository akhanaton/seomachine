### Key Takeaway for ExamPilot Positioning

1. **PMT's weakness is ExamPilot's opportunity:** PMT offers no worked solutions, no AI features, heavy ads, and site reliability issues. Students use it because it's free, not because it's good.
2. **Save My Exams' weakness is pricing:** Students constantly ask "is it worth it?" and compare to free alternatives. The free tier is extremely limited (1 question/month).
3. **The 9709/CIE niche is wide open:** Neither PMT nor SME has deep 9709-specific content strategy. Past paper archives (BestExamHelp, PapaCambridge) dominate but offer zero learning support.
4. **Comparison content is owned by incumbents:** Both SME and PMT publish their own "vs" pages. An independent, honest comparison from ExamPilot's perspective could rank and capture "alternative" traffic.
5. **"Best resources" queries have weak competition:** Mostly teacher blogs and curated link lists. A well-structured comparison article with ratings and criteria would stand out.
