## 3. Student Pain Points (From Forums & Examiner Reports)

### What Students Actually Struggle With

1. **"When do I use which method?"** — The #1 question on Reddit/Student Room. Students report confusion choosing between direct integration, substitution, and reverse chain rule. No existing CIE 9709-specific resource offers a clear decision framework.

2. **Sign errors and forgetting +C** — 9709 examiner reports (2023, 2024) consistently flag omission of the constant of integration and sign errors as the most common integration mistakes.

3. **Areas below the x-axis giving negative values** — Students are caught off guard when integrals produce negative results. They don't know to split the integral and take absolute values.

4. **Reverse chain rule confusion** — Student Room threads show students who learn the shortcut don't understand what they're doing, leading to errors when dividing by expressions containing x.

5. **Setting up integrals from word problems** — Translating "find the area between the curve and the line" into correct limits and integrand is a persistent difficulty.

6. **Integration as "harder differentiation"** — Students comfortable with differentiation struggle because integration requires pattern recognition rather than mechanical rule application.

7. **Not showing working** — Examiner reports note candidates lose marks by using calculators and writing only the final answer without integration steps.
