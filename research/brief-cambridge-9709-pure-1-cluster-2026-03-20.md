## 3. Examiner Report Insights (2024-2025)

From Cambridge Principal Examiner Reports for 9709 Papers 12/13:

### Most Common Mistakes (Content Opportunities)
1. **Insufficient working shown** — students using calculators and writing only answers (marks require method)
2. **Algebraic manipulation errors** — especially with surds, indices, and simplification
3. **Trigonometric identity misapplication** — converting between sin/cos/tan incorrectly
4. **Integration technique selection** — students choosing wrong method for the integral type
5. **Coordinate geometry** — failing to use the discriminant correctly for line/curve intersections
6. **Negative value handling** — sign errors throughout, especially with differentiation of negative powers
7. **Logarithmic identity errors** — particularly with change of base and combining log expressions
8. **Graph sketching** — not showing key features (asymptotes, intercepts, turning points)

**Content opportunity**: A "9709 Common Mistakes" article series covering each topic with examiner report citations would be unique, authoritative, and directly useful.
