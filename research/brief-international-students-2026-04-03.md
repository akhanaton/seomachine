### International Student Pain Points (from forums)
1. **Resource mismatch**: Khan Academy doesn't cover CIE exam style. PMT is Edexcel-only. "I can't find resources for MY exam."
2. **Platform access**: Some UK tools are geo-restricted or UK-payment only
3. **November session ignored**: All revision timelines assume May/June. Nov session students revise during Ramadan/Eid in some years.
4. **Timezone gaps for tutoring**: Students in Asia/Middle East can't access UK-based tutors during their evening study hours
5. **Coaching centre dependency** (Pakistan especially): Students rely on coaching centres but want to supplement with self-study tools
6. **Module selection confusion**: "Which applied module should I choose?" varies by country and school
7. **English as second language**: Maths terminology in English adds cognitive load
