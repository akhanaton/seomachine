## Internal Linking Rules

1. **Every blog post** should link to at least 2 other relevant blog posts
2. **Every blog post** should include at least 1 CTA link (waitlist or feature page)
3. **Topic pages** should link to the relevant hub/pillar page
4. **Hub pages** should link to all child topic pages
5. **Never link to the same URL more than twice** in one article
6. **Use descriptive anchor text** — "Cambridge 9709 Pure 1 revision guide" not "click here"
