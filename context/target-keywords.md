## Launch Focus: Cambridge 9709 Pure Mathematics 1

### Exam Papers
- **9709/11** — Pure Mathematics 1 (variant 1)
- **9709/12** — Pure Mathematics 1 (variant 2)  
- **9709/13** — Pure Mathematics 1 (variant 3)

### Core Topics (Paper 1)

| Topic | Key Subtopics |
|-------|---------------|
| **1.1 Quadratics** | Completing the square, discriminant, solving equations/inequalities, simultaneous equations |
| **1.2 Functions** | Domain, range, one-one functions, inverse functions, composition, transformations |
| **1.3 Coordinate Geometry** | Straight lines, circles, intersection problems, tangents and normals |
| **1.4 Circular Measure** | Radians, arc length, sector area |
| **1.5 Trigonometry** | Sin/cos/tan graphs, exact values, inverse trig, identities, solving equations |
| **1.6 Series** | Binomial expansion, arithmetic progressions, geometric progressions, sum to infinity |
| **1.7 Differentiation** | Gradient as limit, chain rule, stationary points, rates of change |
| **1.8 Integration** | Reverse differentiation, definite integrals, area under curves |

> **Note**: Vectors is NOT in Pure 1. It is topic 3.7 in Pure Mathematics 3 (Paper 3). Confirmed from the official 2026-2027 syllabus.

### Prior Knowledge Assumed
- Cambridge IGCSE Mathematics 0580 (Extended) or O Level 4024/4029
- Surd manipulation (e.g., √12 = 2√3)
- Graph shapes for y = kx^n
