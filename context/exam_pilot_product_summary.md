# ExamPilot Product Summary

## What is ExamPilot?

ExamPilot is an AI-powered exam readiness assessment platform that helps students maximize their exam performance through intelligent, adaptive practice and personalized learning insights.

## Core Value Proposition

**Know exactly where you stand and what to do next to improve your predicted grade.**

## Our Approach

ExamPilot delivers exam confidence through a comprehensive, evidence-based learning cycle:

**Assess** — We evaluate your current exam readiness through our PRISM architecture, leveraging Question DNA to precisely identify knowledge gaps and misconceptions across the entire syllabus.

**Diagnose** — Our intelligent analysis pinpoints exactly where you need support, revealing not just what you got wrong, but why — uncovering the underlying misconceptions that hold you back. (Taxonomy, Knowledge Graph, Curriculum, Misconceptions)

**Close Gaps** — We deliver targeted practice with the exact questions you need, supported by AI-powered Socratic teaching that guides you to deep understanding rather than surface-level memorization. Mastery-based progression ensures you only advance when truly ready, building confidence and competence together. (Knowledge and Learner Profiles)

**Retain** — Our FSRS-powered spaced repetition system ensures long-term retention, strategically surfacing content at optimal intervals so you don't forget what you've learned. (FSRS)

**Predict** — Through continuous assessment and adaptive challenge, we provide a high-confidence predicted grade that gives you clarity on your exam readiness and a clear roadmap to improvement. Personalized feedback and achievement tracking keep you motivated throughout your learning journey. (Exam Readiness Index)

The result: **confidence in your preparedness**, backed by data-driven insights and a personalized path to your target grade.

## How It Works

### 1. **Intelligent Assessment**
We assess your exam readiness using Question DNA — the fundamental reasoning patterns that underpin exam questions. Your responses reveal:
- **Misconceptions** you hold
- **Content coverage gaps** in required syllabus material
- **Memory retention** through spaced repetition (FSRS)
- **Mastery levels** across different topics and question types

### 2. **Exam Readiness Index (ERI)**
Your performance data feeds into a comprehensive **Exam Readiness Index (ERI)** — a predictive metric that estimates your current grade trajectory and identifies specific areas for improvement.

### 3. **Adaptive Challenge**
Questions adapt to your ability level:
- Start at your current mastery level
- Progressively increase in difficulty
- Reach exam-standard difficulty when you're ready
- Ensure you're challenged appropriately without being overwhelmed

### 4. **Personalized Learning**
Every interaction is tailored to you:
- **AI-assisted explanations** based on your knowledge profile and learning preferences (MSLQ)
- **Targeted practice** on your weak areas
- **Spaced repetition** to strengthen long-term memory retention
- **Custom question selection** aligned with your knowledge gaps

### 5. **ExamLens: Predictive Mock Exams**
Our **ExamLens** feature generates predictive mock exams based on your Question DNA mastery data:
- Realistic exam simulations
- Questions matched to your current ability and gaps
- Performance predictions aligned with your ERI
- Actionable insights on what to revise before the real exam
